Why You Should Recycle

Over 250 million tons of trash is produced in the United States every year. According to theworldcounts.com, there is approximately 40 million tons of electronic waste generated by the world every year and seventy percent of our toxic waste comes from electronics. Improper disposal of electronics can lead to toxic waste such as lead and mercury being released into the environment. As a computer retail and service provider, we at Computer World have made it part of our mission to help reduce electronic waste and prevent these toxins from doing further damage to the environment.





Recycling with Computer World

If you wish to take advantage of our recycling service, please complete the form on the right.Upon submitting the form, we will send you a confirmation email including a label to print and include with your items. We would appreciate any information about the items provided to us via the notes/comments box. Upon receiving the items to be recycled, we will break them down into materials in an environmentally friendly way to be recycled into new parts. We appreciate your concern for the environment. Thank you for considering our recycling service, we hope you decide to recycle with us!
